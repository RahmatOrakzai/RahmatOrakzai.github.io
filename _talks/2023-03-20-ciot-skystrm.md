Conference presentation describing the Skystrm activity monitoring system, which uses computer vision techniques to analyse movement patterns of older adults and detect deviations from normal behaviour to support independent living and remote caregiving.

**Authors:** Ikram Asghar, Rahmat Ullah, Mark G. Griffiths, Gareth Evans, Justus Vermaak
